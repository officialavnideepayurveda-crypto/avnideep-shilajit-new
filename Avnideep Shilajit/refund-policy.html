Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: January 2026

10 Days Money Back Guarantee — agar aap product se 100% satisfied nahi hain toh hum aapka pura paisa wapas karenge (T&C apply).

1. Eligibility for Refund

  • Product 10 din ke andar return karna hoga
  • Product sealed aur original packaging mein hona chahiye
  • Order ID aur purchase proof zaroori hai
  • Refund request support team ko email/call karke karein

2. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Opened/used products (hygiene reasons)
  • 10 din ke baad ka return
  • Damaged due to customer negligence

3. Refund Process

  1. Support ko call/email karein refund request ke liye
  2. Hum pickup arrange karenge (free of cost)
  3. Product quality check ke baad refund initiate hoga
  4. Prepaid: 5-7 working days mein source account mein
  5. COD: Bank transfer ya UPI ke through 7-10 days mein

4. Damaged/Wrong Product

Agar aapko damaged ya wrong product mile, toh 48 ghante ke andar humein contact karein. Unboxing video helpful hota hai. Hum free replacement bhejenge.

5. Order Cancellation

Order dispatch hone se pehle cancel kar sakte hain. Dispatch ke baad cancellation possible nahi hai — but return policy use kar sakte hain.
