Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Avnideep Ayurveda aapko fast aur discreet shipping provide karta hai. Yeh policy shipping time, delivery details aur packet handling ke baare mein batati hai.

1. Shipping Area

Hum India ke sabhi regions mein shipping karte hain. Aapka order aapke registered address par deliver kiya jayega.

2. Delivery Time

  • Standard delivery: 3-7 business days
  • Remote areas mein 1-2 extra din lag sakte hain
  • Order confirmation ke baad delivery tracking details provide ki jayegi

3. Discreet Packaging

Aapka product discreet packaging mein aata hai. Package par product ka naam nahi likha jata, isliye privacy guaranteed hai.

4. Free Shipping

Hamare most orders par free shipping available hai. Koi hidden charges nahi lagte.

5. Address Changes

Agar aapka address change hota hai, turant support se contact karein. Delivery dispatch ke baad address change karna mushkil hota hai.

6. Shipping Delays

Kabhi kabhi severe weather, courier network issues, busy seasons ya public holidays ke wajah se delay ho sakta hai. Aise cases mein hum aapko update karenge.
